Verify it yourself

The strongest privacy proof is the one you confirm in your own browser:

  1. Open DevTools (Cmd+Option+I on Mac, F12 on Windows).
  2. Switch to the Network tab. Click the × to clear.
  3. Click Re-run tests above.
  4. Watch the Network tab. Nothing should fire to any remote host.

Once Steve.ai's local-only mode is the default (phase 6 of the v2 rebuild), every interaction in the app will pass this same test. Until then, captures still flow through the existing Supabase path unless you enable local-only mode in Settings.
