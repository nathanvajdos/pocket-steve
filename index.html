How to get a share URL — step by step

iCloud (iPhone):

  1. Open the Calendar app on your iPhone
  2. Tap Calendars at the bottom
  3. Tap the (i) info icon next to the calendar you want me to watch
  4. Toggle Public Calendar ON (the URL is non-guessable; nobody can find it without you sending it)
  5. Tap Share Link → Copy
  6. Come back here and paste below

Google Calendar (calendar.google.com):

  1. Click the gear icon → Settings
  2. Pick the calendar from the left rail → Integrate calendar
  3. Copy Secret address in iCal format

Outlook (without OAuth): The "Connect Outlook" button above is the better path — but if you want a URL: outlook.office.com → Settings → Calendar → Shared calendars → Publish a calendar → copy the ICS link.
